Matter is the stuff that everything is made up of. All matter has mass and volume. It has physical properties which can be changed by the appearance only and has chemical properties which a substance can be changed into something different that can't be undone. Energy can be transferred but never be destroyed. There are two type of energy, kinetic and potential. Kinetic is energy that an object has as it moves. Potential energy is the energy it has do to its position or shape.
